Opsins are proteins that give your cells certain superpowers. But: each opsin is diffent. (In our case) some give you the ability to quickly jump very high and some make you jump very slowly or only very shallow. Find the correct superpowers (opsin) that allow you to catch all targets.

Who get's the superpowers? (viral vectors) üîë

To give cells optogenetic superpowers we have to get access to them by using viral vectors. They are like keys that can unlock cells and thus define who gets superpowers and who doesn't. Some viral vectors are quite specific and only unlock single cells, some are like general keys and unlock several cells. Find the right viral vector "key" that only unlocks (and gives the superpowers to) the neuron, not the enemy.
